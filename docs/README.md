# OctoAcme Project Management Documentation

Welcome to the OctoAcme Project Management docs! This folder centralizes all institutional knowledge, playbooks, and decision rationale for project management at OctoAcme.

## Project Management Process Overview

OctoAcme's project management is built on a structured, repeatable framework ensuring clarity, consistency, and collaborative success:

- **Initiation:** Define objectives, stakeholders, success metrics, and secure approvals.
- **Planning:** Detail timelines, deliverables, schedules, risks, and resource allocation.
- **Execution & Tracking:** Monitor progress, adapt plans, manage changes, and maintain transparency with stakeholders.
- **Risk & Communication:** Proactively identify, escalate, and address risks; communicate clearly and timely.
- **Release & Deployment:** Govern handoff procedures, testing, and go-live processes.
- **Retrospective & Improvement:** Conduct post-project review to drive continuous improvement.
- **Roles & Personas:** Clearly define responsibilities, handoffs, and accountabilities for each team role.
